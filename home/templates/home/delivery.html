Delivery Policy

"Getting your order to you is our top priority. Getting it to you on time is our second priority. Getting it to you in one piece is a close third."

Delivery Times

We dispatch all orders within 2-3 working days. Delivery times thereafter depend on your location within the galaxy:

Destination Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Earth 7-14 working days €4.99
Milkyway 200-300 working days €19.99
Outer Spiral Arms of the Galaxy Please allow several million years €99.99

Free Delivery

We offer free delivery on all orders over €50. Because if you are spending that much on books about the galaxy, the least we can do is cover the postage.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Please note that tracking information may not update immediately, particularly if your parcel is passing through a region of unusual space-time curvature.

Damaged or Lost Orders

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive at all, please contact us within 14 days and we will do our best to help. Please do not panic. There is almost certainly a perfectly reasonable explanation.

International Customs

Orders delivered outside the EU may be subject to customs charges. These are the responsibility of the recipient. Megadodo Publications cannot be held responsible for any delays caused by planetary customs officials, intergalactic trade disputes or Vogon bureaucracy.

The Towel

Towels are dispatched separately from books and may arrive on a different day. We recommend always knowing where your towel is, so please check your tracking information carefully.
